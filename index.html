Mark Zuckerberg

Education

  1. Graduated from White Plains High School in 1999
  2. In 1996, 12-year-old Mark Zuckerberg created the first full-fledged software product called ZuckNet, which allowed family members to communicate over a local network.
  3. His passion for programming began to manifest itself in middle school.
    By the ninth grade, he released a computer version of the board game Risk,
    which was very popular at the time.
  4. After high school, went to Harvard University, where he was fond of hacking.
    He created a student website that evaluated photographs.
    This was the beginning of the creation of the social network Facebook

About yourself



He speaks Mandarin Chinese, which he learned to communicate with his wife's grandmother and to promote his business ideas in China. He is a member of the Supervisory Board of the School of Economics and Management at Peking University. He has met twice with Chinese President Xi Jinping, and during the last meeting he spoke to him in Mandarin.



Work experience

  • Facebook

    • Joining forces with friends, Zuckerberg creates a social network, initially called "The Facebook," which allows users to create profiles, upload photos, and communicate with each other. After completing his sophomore year, Mark quit his studies to devote himself entirely to the site and moved the company to Palo Alto, California. By the end of 2004, Facebook had already reached 1 million users.

  • Microsoft

    • For his graduation thesis, he created a program to recognize the musical tastes of Internet users - Synapse, which Microsoft later wanted to buy for $2 million. The management of the giant IT company offered to hire him as an employee until he graduated, but he refused both remuneration and cooperation, saying that "inspiration is not for sale."
